Easy Fried Rice

How to Make Easy Fried Rice

Making fried rice is a breeze with the recipe you'll find below. The process is simple - preheat your pan before swirling in oil and aromatics, then add uncooked proteins if applicable. Add in rice and vegetables, vigorously tossing ingredients for that toasty, golden-brown color that signals big flavor.

You'll find detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instruction in the recipe below:

Easy Fried Rice Ingredients:

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this easy fried rice recipe:

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Step-by-step Instruction for A Delicious Easy Fried Rice:

  1. Assemble all ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. And eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
